False Prophets
 
Posted in this partition is my interpretation of the parable of the fig tree that was cursed by the earthlty messiah on his way to Jerusalem because it had leaves, but not fruit. I believe that interpretation to be correct, but some are offended by it; and one very nearly accused me of being a false prophet. My direct reply weas " A false prophet would have had evil figs. This tree had none. Now, the scripture does speak of evil fits, and your interpretation bears consideration in that context."

That reply is a little snarky, but I let it pass, but I couldn't let the accusation pass. Rather than offer tit for tat, as warring countries do, I posted the following on my personal page (giving space for the waters to heal):

I had reason to think of false prophets, recently. When my mind goes there, it tends to land somewhere in the Bible Belt. Now, that's pretty close to an accusation, and we know the accuser of the brethren is cast down.

I'll put a foot in the ditch with that accuser, by giving an opinion. I admit that I fudge the bet since I warn you it's an opinion, but warning seemed appropriate.

Earth has had some bad shakeups in the past: there are wonders just off the coasts, popping up from the ground, occasionally, being stripped of vegetation, etc. But the shake-up coming on this moment of history is to be worse than any before or any after. We've had the nuclear meltdowns in Russia and Japan, the oil geyser in the Gulf of Mexico. Fires, floods, cyclones, earthquakes, wars. These are bad, but. No, let me say BUT!

In my OpinioN, New Jerusalem coming down from heaven like a bride all prepared? Fulfillment of that prophecy is within each of us. BuT, I'll say this about the effects of that vision: for there is both prophecy and the effects of every vision.

New Jerusalem as EFFECT (Oh! How we hedge our bets!) is Antarctica. The ice is her wedding dress, It's melting, and what's underneath is coming AS down from heaven.

But for the elect's sake, those days are shortened. The elect, the inheritors of Earth, will have to have a place to go while the waters and the ground everywhere else on Earth are healed from the ravages of the last 2024 years
Ah! The treasures of the ice and snow. Beautiful for situation: on the sides of the North. Only one place on Earth is on the sides of the North, and that place is the South Pole.

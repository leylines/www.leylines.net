Although the nine-point star is implied by the properties of the vertical Adam Kadmons, the actual image is derived from the cubit of the lateral symbols of the Tree of Life. It is produced by the same measurements that produced the stars of the seven churches of Asia, except that those measurements are applied to stars drawn with the horizontal Adam Kadmons. When the resulting angles are circumscribed and the lateral cubits are distributed around their circumferences, other polygons will appear, as demonstrated above.

This lateral series of diagrams will add to the vertical perspective the sense of a spiral movement. These things recall the interpretation of  "Havilah" as "the downward spiral." Bypassed on the way to Nod, that scriptural land, as reflected within each of us, is worthy of revisiting in its appointed time.

Within the land of Havilah are gold, spice, and semi-precious jewels; but the waters flow on from the Garden to water the whole earth. Thus, this series of measurements has been noted, but not particularly explored. As implied by the message to the angelic seat at Laodicea, there is more to come, even for those who have overcome! Eye hath not see, nor ear heard, as it's written.

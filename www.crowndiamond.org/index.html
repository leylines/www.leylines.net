My love is like a
gazelle or a young stag:
Behold! he stands
behind our wall,
gazing in at the windows,
showing himself
through the lattice.		 And he made for the altar
a grating, a network
under its ledge to its
middle. And he cast four
rings for the four ends
of the grate of brass, to be
places for the staves.

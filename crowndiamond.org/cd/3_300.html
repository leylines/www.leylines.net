Hypertext Gematria & Numerology Tables
 

These pages present the gematria and numerology of biblical word forms, with interpretation based upon the Paleo-Hebrew script.
Standard Aramaic definitions are given for each word form, along with the notaricon of each word value, both as expressed in traditionalgematria value and in numerological value.
Gematria values are prefaced with the letter g; and numerical values, with the letter n.

Click on the appropriate values below to find the page for the word that is of interest. Prefixed and suffixed word forms are not generally included in these tables at present.
To view the Hebrew word forms, you will need to download and install the font appropriate to your platform by selecting the
download-to-disk option at http://www.crowndiamond.org/public/Fonts/.

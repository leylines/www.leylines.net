Its Peoples and its Times

I will magnify Torah,
and make it glorious.
The names and times of Nod drew my thoughts into a subtext drawn, not from words, but from spellings of words as alphanumeric emblems. The question is not whether these assertions are true, but whether they witness to Truth.

A mystic from my youth, I recently received the book, as though in a dream. I look forward to learning from the experience of adapting the presentation to this forum.

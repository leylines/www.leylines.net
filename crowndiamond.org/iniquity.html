The Mystery of Iniquity
 
 
The name Lucifer means "Light Bearer." The covering cherub of heaven, he was perfect in all his ways until iniquity was found in him. It is commonly understood that "iniquity" is "grossly unfair or immoral behavior." If that's the case, his perfection was conditional on his performance: his to achieve and maintain, and his to lose; and the inquity found in him was generated by a narcissistic pride: "Pride goes before destruction, and a haughty spirit before a fall."

My view is that the imperfection discovered in Lucifer was due to a bending of the light he carried, not because of some fault of character. His fall came after the creation of Earth; for his judgment was to be cast down to Earth to live and die as Man, subsequent to the discovery iniquity, which must have "crept into him, unawares," to borrow the scriptural phrase.

The light he carried within himself was bent by the pull of inertia. The force of gravity accompanies all mass in Creation. Iniquity is the spiritual equivalent of the latent force of inertia: gravity, which has the power to bend light. His fall wasn't due to sin or error, precisely; but to the effects of the essential cohesive element of the universe. There would be no accretion of mass without inertia: no Earth, no life.

The pull of Creation bent the light Lucifer carried within himself. Because of this effect of iniquity, he was cast from the immortal realm to the mortal realm; and the loss of the heavenly state was a severe change, but it was not tragic. It is written that all things in the strange work of Father YH work together for good.The angels shouted for joy at the creation of man; for mankind would be the vehicle for the tranformation of angels from *bearers* of light by their recreation as *beings* of Light. This mystery is among the things the early believers were unable to bear: not because of some failing or weakness, but because of the sequential aspect of knowledge. One must understand "this" in order to be able to understand "that."

Thus, the apostle laments, "the mystery of iniquity does already work." Stumbles among believers were apparent. The path of the cross must lead to knowledge of the intersection of the mortal and immortal realms. Those born of the flesh who truly walk in the steps of Y'shua are born of the Holy Breath, the Holy Spirit. Far greater than the light found in natural man, the light in within spiritual man is the very essence of the Godhead. Even so, they must carry this treasure in earthen vessels.

Living the life of the Spirit in mortal bodies is a dynamic experience, and the body mass opf physical man exerts an equivalent of the latent force of inertia upon the new creature, the spiritual man. This is why Paul saw two laws operating within himself, and why the scripture speaks of twins struggling within the womb.It is written, "the end shall not come, except there be a falling away, first."

The phoenix once flew near the Source of Light, but burned to the ground at his first approach. Having fallen back to Earth, it was incumbent for it to rise again. A roughly equivalent experience unfolds as natural man, reborn of the Holy Breath as spiritual man, joins in the procession to the great wedding feast of the Messiah of Matthew 1:1 in the steps of the man of Matthew 1:16. In this awakend state, believers are able to add knowledge to experience as they, again, appoach the gates of Heaven.

It is inevitable that the new creature rising from the waters of baptism should stumble. Offense will come, and woe to them who are the cause of it; but mercy and restoration to those who must experience it.

If we say we are without sin we deceive ourselves and the truth is not in us; but if we confess our errors-- our sins, the Holy Father is faithful to forgive us and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness.The filthy rags of unrighteoussness will be cleansed not in the pew, but by being put to use in the world's ditches, as those walking in Messiah lend helping hands to fallen brothers. If believers cordon themselves off from others they perceive to be unrighteous in order to protect their sense of their own righteousness, they will discover their lamps lack oil as the great wedding feast comes nearer with every darkening day of Earth.

By this you shall know that you are disciples of Master Y'shua: that you have love for one another: and not only for others you see as good, but for all who live.

Judge not.

Accuse not.

Open to love.
